ABOUT ME

Current Work In Progress

My name is Kien Dang and I am a self-motivated learner with an Honours Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Biochemistry with co-operative education from the University of Waterloo. During my co-op terms, I had the pleasure in working in various job environments that include the Canadian Cancer Society, Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada and The Woodbridge Group. Co-operative education has given me the essential skills to succeed in many different job environments ranging from extensive lab experience that were in compliance with ISO and ASTM standards and handling multiple research projects as well as writing many SOPs for the required tasks. My current goal is to learn different programming languages to benefit my career choices in the future.

Kien Dang


I have always been fascinated with science and technology. Studying biochemistry has given me the foundational knowledge in understanding how life works, thus, preparing me to pursue a career that can enrich our quality of life through biochemical research, biotechnology or genetic engineering. Technology is often the consequence of science and engineering and interacting with my first computer in 2003 has always sparked interest in computers for me. Figuring out how computers work and how they do things to improve my quality of life was one of my favourite things to do. Now with a deeper understanding of computer science, I hope that I will be able to find the balance in science and in technology to help improve my quality of work as well as help others through technology itself. Computer programming will be one of my strongest tool for biochemistry work and for potential careers in computer technology.
